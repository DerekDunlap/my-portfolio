Sports Bros.

Project Info:

Contributed towards a group project that led toward the development of a Sports News website. Allows visitors of the site to see the 'Top Headline News' across all sports, gather news based on the desired league, see live scores and upcoming schedules of games for the available sports, search NBA Players career stats lines; without the unnecessary distractions that exist in other major sports news sites.
